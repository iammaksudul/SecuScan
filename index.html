SecuScan - cPanel Compromise Detection

This plugin scans your cPanel accounts for suspicious activities, such as compromised files, failed login attempts, and other signs of a potential compromise. It helps you stay secure and take actions when necessary.

Features

  • Scan Apache logs for suspicious POST requests and eval usage
  • Detect suspicious files in public_html directories
  • Check failed SSH login attempts in server logs
  • Monitor cron jobs and .htaccess files for unauthorized modifications
  • Send email alerts to admin for compromised accounts
  • Provide detailed logs and results of scans
